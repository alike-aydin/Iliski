Iliski means relationsip in Turkish. As it happens, Iliski helps you compute the dynamical relationship between two timeseries, namely a Transfer Function.

Iliski has been developed in S. Charpak's team, under the Creative Commons CC BY 4.0 License (see LICENSE.txt or https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Last version is available on GitLab (https://gitlab.com/AliK_A/iliski), with the User Manual.
